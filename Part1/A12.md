# Faraday bag

Blocks all wireless signals to/from devices preventing wireless access
to the devices.

# Paper shredder

Destroys sensitive documents preserving the security of the 
information that may be found on the sensitive documents.

# Hardware-encrypted USB drive

Stores data securely without internet by the usb maintaining its 
encryption itself preventing access to the data. 


# Physical lockbox/safe 

Protects valuables and documents by preventing access from a third party
without a key or knowledge of the code needed to open the lockbox or safe.

# CCTV

Records footage offline preventing a third party from accessing the footage
while ensuring that any actions performed in the survelled area can be 
seen.
