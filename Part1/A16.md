# Sydney University Data Breach

Source: https://www.cyberdaily.au/security/13040-sydney-university-hacked-over-13-000-impacted?utm_source=CyberDaily&utm_campaign=18_12_2025&utm_medium=email&utm_content=Newsflash&utm_emailID=fe740e8223e19c88ab5a9844d8dad2669ae410a794b23cad1d082072530b5507

Hackers accessed a database used for code storage and development, but some historical 
records were being stored in the same database leading to the exfiltration of the 
personal information of 13000 staff and former students

# Western Sydney University Data Breach

Source: https://www.cyberdaily.au/security/11301-western-sydney-university-suffers-cyber-attack-personal-data-compromised

The threat actor gained access to an IT account with permissions to the universities 
student management system. Through this, they were able to gain access to information 
about students enrollment information among the standard personal information.

# Qantas Airline Data Breach

Source: https://www.cyberdaily.au/security/11207-qantas-customer-passports-at-risk-following-frequent-flyer-cyber-theft

Employees that were apart of india SATS were able to gain access to airline bookings,
leading to the wiring of frequent flyer points to a private account that was controlled
by the employees. With this, the passport information of customers may have been 
compromised, possibly leading to up to 1000 passports being leaked.
