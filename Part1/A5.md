# Off-the-record messaging

OTR messaging takes advantage of deniable authentication by avoiding the use of digital signatures, meaning that no message can be confirmed to be sent by one of the 2 participants since a third party can forge messages. Instead, per-message AE5 keys are used and exchanged using Diffie-Hellman, this means that any compromise of long-live keys will not lead to the compromise of any previous or future messages.
