# Hashing

Hashing refers to a mathmatical procedure in which data is manipulatedto prevent 
the reversal and discovery of the inital input data. Some algorithms 
that are commonly used are SHA-256, MD5 and CRC-32, each of these having different
strengths and weaknesses. 

The reason for them being "irreversible" is due to how difficult it is to preform
the algorithms in reverse compared to forward due to their use of modular
arithmatic, bitwise shifts, and logical operations.
